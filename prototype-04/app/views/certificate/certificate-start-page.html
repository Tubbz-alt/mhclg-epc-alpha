Find an energy certificate

Use this service to find:

  • energy performance certificates (EPC)
  • display energy certificates (DEC) for public authority buildings
  • air conditioning inspection certificates (AC-CERT) for systems over 12kW

Before you start

You will need the postcode for the property.

It will help if you know the type of certificate you are looking for. The type depends on the use of the property. This guide explains the different type of energy performance certificates.

If an owner or occupier of a property opted out of the EPC register, the property information can only be found using the reference number on the certificate.

You can opt out of the EPC register if you do not want other people to see your EPC.
