Find an energy certificate

Use this service to find:

  • energy performance certificates (EPCs)
  • display energy certificates (DEC) for public authority buildings
  • air conditioning inspection certificates (AC-CERT) for air conditioning systems over 12kW

If an owner or occupier of a property opted out of the EPC register, the property information can only be found using the reference number on the certificate. You can opt out of the EPC register if you do not want other people to see your EPC.

This service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).
