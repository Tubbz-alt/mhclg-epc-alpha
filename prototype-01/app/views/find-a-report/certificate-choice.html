Help with certificate types
Energy Performance Certificates (EPCs) are for domestic and non-domestic buildings and are required as part of the buying, selling and renting process.
Display Energy Certificates (DEC) are for public authority buildings these certificates are displayed on public buildings to show the energy performance of the building over the past 12 months.
Air conditioning inspection certificates (AC-CERT) are for buildings with air conditioning systems over 12KW.
If you are looking for a specific certificate type select yes. Select no to show all certificates for that postcode. Select All to see all certificates.
