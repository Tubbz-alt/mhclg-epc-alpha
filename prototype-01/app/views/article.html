Energy performance of buildings register

Welcome to the Energy Performance of Buildings Register

The Register includes EPCs for non-domestic buildings, Display Energy Certificates for public authority buildings and air conditioning inspection reports for air conditioning systems over 12kW.

An Energy Performance Certificate is required for properties when constructed, sold or let. The Energy Performance Certificate provides details on the energy performance of the property and what you can do to improve it.

Find a Report

Find an EPC using the property's postcode or the 24-digit Report Reference Number. This is printed on the report or provided by the Energy Assessor who produced it.

Find a Report

Find an Assessor

To get an EPC for your home, you need to have your property assessed. Use this service to search for an accredited assessor in your area.

You can also use this service to check the accreditation of an individual assessor.

Find an Assessor

Opt in or opt out

As part of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) you can opt to have your address removed from the Register. If you have previously removed your details you can opt to have your details included.
