Find an Energy Performance Certificate

Use this service to:

  • Energy Performance Certificates (EPCs) for domestic and non-domestic buildings.
  • Display Energy Certificates (DEC) for public authority buildings.
  • Air conditioning inspection certificates (AC-CERT) for air conditioning systems over 12kW.
! Warning If the owner and/or occupier of the property has chosen to remove a property from general searches, the property information can only be found using the reference number on the certificate.
