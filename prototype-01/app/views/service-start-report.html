Find an energy certificate

Use this service to find:

  • energy performance certificates (EPCs) for residential and non-residential buildings
  • display energy certificates (DEC) for public authority buildings
  • air conditioning inspection certificates (AC-CERT) for air conditioning systems over 12kW
! Warning If the owner and/or occupier of the property has chosen to remove a property from general searches, the property information can only be found using the reference number on the certificate.
Start now

Before you start

You will need the postcode for the property.

It will help if you know the type of certificate you are looking for. The type depends on the use of the property. This guide explains the different type of energy performance certificates.

The online service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).
