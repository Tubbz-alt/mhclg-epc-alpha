Find an energy assessor

To get an energy performance certificate for your home, you need to have your property assessed. Use this service to search for an accredited assessor in your area.

Note that energy assessors are qualified to carry out particular types of assessment and you should check that they are qualified to carry out the appropriate type of assessment for your property.

Start now

Before you start

When you find a local assessor:

  • Check that they are part of an accredited scheme.
  • Contact them directly to arrange an assessment.
  • For a business property search for a Commercial Energy Assessor.
